## Project Contribution Overview

This trimester, my major contribution was building a large portion of the system that creates and manages student teams, including:

- Groups Management interface (Pages frontend), which is a workflow to create groups, search groups, filter by period/course, manage membership
- A new AI-powered grouping tool that can generate “best-fit” teams using persona compatibility
- A feedback feature so grouping quality is based on past experiences of groups, student ratings, teacher ratings, and one-line explanations (why the group worked or didn’t)

This was necessary and impactful for OCS because it helps us craft new persona-based groups to introduce diversity of thought into project-based learning. When groups are made with multiple personas that are predicted to work well together, people may branch away from sitting with their friends and may instead form more functional, productive groups.
